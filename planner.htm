Welcome to Plan-A-Move planner page!

Use this page as your personal planner to help ensure you don't forget anything on your next move.

To add an item to your list, click on the "Add Items" icon on the bottom right of this page, add a title of your note and a brief description and click "Save".

You can view your notes by clicking on the "Display Items" icon on the bottom left of this page.
