Planning Instructions

If you’re wondering how to prepare for a move, you’ve come to the right place! We at Plan-A-Move understand that the laundry list of things to do when moving can be overwhelming. We are here to help!

Getting organized is the key.

Our Moving Supplies page will provide a list of supplies to consider when moving. Repairs Cleaning will suggest things to be cleaned and/or repaired prior to your move. Utilities Page will list utility companies in your area that may need to be notified of your move. Moving Companies will give you a list of companies you may consider helping you move. Notifications page will provide a list of people and companies that may need to be notified of your move. Finally, Schedule page will help you get organized and specify what needs to be done in what order to get you moving.
